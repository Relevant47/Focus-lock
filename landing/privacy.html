Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Short version: FocusLock runs entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our servers — not your browsing habits, blocked sites, session history, or anything else. The only external communication is to validate your license key (via Lemon Squeezy) and to check for software updates (via GitHub Releases).

1. What data we collect

FocusLock collects no personal data. All application data — your focus profiles, session history, analytics, and settings — is stored locally on your device in:

  • Windows: %ProgramData%\FocusLock\
  • macOS: /Library/Application Support/FocusLock/

This data never leaves your device except as described below.

2. License validation

When you activate a license key, FocusLock sends the following to Lemon Squeezy's API (api.lemonsqueezy.com):

  • Your license key
  • An anonymised instance identifier (a random string generated on your device)

We do not send your name, email address (it remains on your device), IP address, or any app usage data. Lemon Squeezy's privacy policy applies to this interaction: lemonsqueezy.com/privacy.

3. Software updates

FocusLock periodically checks for updates by contacting releases.focuslock.app and the GitHub API. This request includes:

  • Your operating system and CPU architecture (e.g. "windows-x86_64")
  • Your current FocusLock version number

No personally identifiable information is included. Your IP address is visible to our update server as part of the normal HTTP request, but it is not logged or stored.

4. Crash reporting and telemetry

FocusLock has no crash reporting, telemetry, or analytics. We receive no data about how you use the application, which sites you block, or how long your sessions are.

5. Payment information

Payments are processed by Lemon Squeezy. FocusLock never sees your credit card number or any payment details. Refer to Lemon Squeezy's privacy policy for how they handle payment data.

6. Children's privacy

FocusLock is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above and notify users through the app's update mechanism.

8. Contact

Privacy questions: privacy@focuslock.app
